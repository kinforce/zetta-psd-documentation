How To Edit PSD File

In order to edit the file, you need to have Adobe Photoshop installed on your system, and the fonts mentioned in the credits installed. The layers are well organized and grouped into folders, which make editing very easy. So, all you have to do, is select the layer from the layers window, and then edit it with Adobe Photoshop.

That's all! Enjoy.
